1 Foreground, warmed up
The metaphor drives every choice: one ink panel holds the foreground; everything behind it recedes into the sand. The score arithmetic is set as a receipt-style ledger — adds, then a divide, then the staleness multiplier, then the equation and its answer — so the math reads top-to-bottom without a table. Terracotta is reserved for time pressure and primary actions, sage for flow (unblocks, momentum, “touch it”), and true red appears only on genuinely overdue deadlines. Every text pair below was contrast-checked to ≥4.5:1 in both themes; targets are ≥44px.
